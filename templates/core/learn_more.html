About Purbanchal Roadways

Your Trusted Logistics Partner Since 1997

Company Overview

Purbanchal Roadways is a well-established transport and logistics company headquartered in Assam, India, with over 25 years of experience in the industry. The company has built a strong reputation for reliability and efficiency in handling diverse transportation needs, particularly in challenging terrains like northeast India and hilly regions.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, efficient, and cost-effective logistics solutions that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of safety and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be the preferred logistics partner in India, known for our expertise in handling complex transportation challenges and our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Key Services Offered

Multimodal Project Logistics

End-to-end transportation solutions combining road, rail, and other logistics modes to ensure efficient delivery of your goods across diverse terrains and locations.

Over Dimensional Cargo (ODC) & Heavy Lift Transport

Specialized handling of oversized and overweight cargo with custom equipment and expert planning to ensure safe and compliant transportation.

Hilly Terrain Transportation

Expertise in moving heavy and oversized loads in difficult terrains, including the northeastern states, with specialized vehicles and experienced drivers.

Industrial & Infrastructure Logistics

Transport services for hydroelectric projects, power grid installations, and other industrial sectors requiring specialized handling and equipment.

Household Goods & Car Transportation

Safe and secure relocation services for personal and commercial vehicles, with comprehensive insurance coverage and real-time tracking.

Packing & Moving Services

Comprehensive packing, loading, and unloading solutions for domestic and commercial moves across India, with professional handling of all types of goods.
