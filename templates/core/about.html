Purbanchal Roadways History

Established in 1997, Purbanchal Roadways has grown from a small local transport service to one of the most trusted logistics companies in Assam and beyond. With over 25 years of experience in the industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.

Our journey began in Dibrugarh, Assam, with a vision to provide quality transport services to the people of Northeast India. Over the years, we have expanded our operations to cover the entire country, serving thousands of satisfied customers.

Mission

At Purbanchal Roadways, our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and efficient logistics solutions that meet the diverse needs of our customers. We are committed to delivering exceptional service at competitive prices, ensuring that your goods reach their destination safely and on time.

We strive to be the preferred logistics partner for individuals and businesses across India, known for our professionalism, integrity, and customer-centric approach.
